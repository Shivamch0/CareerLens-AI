
CAREERLENS 🚀

CAREERLENS is a full‑lifecycle career guidance platform designed to help students and professionals make smarter education and career decisions through stage‑aware recommendations, skill gap analysis, and personalized roadmaps.

It supports users from Class 12 → Bachelor's → Master's → Job readiness with structured insights, tools, and actionable learning paths.


---

🌟 Vision

CAREERLENS aims to become a single intelligent platform for career decision‑making, helping users:

Discover suitable career paths

Identify skill gaps

Build structured learning roadmaps

Improve job readiness

Prepare resumes and interviews



---

🎯 Problem Statement

Students often struggle with:

Choosing the right degree after Class 12

Selecting career paths after graduation

Understanding required skills for roles

Preparing industry‑ready resumes

Practicing interviews effectively


CAREERLENS solves these challenges with data‑driven recommendations + guided workflows.


---

🧠 Core Features

1️⃣ Smart Onboarding

Collects user details such as:

Current education stage

Interests

Strength areas

Career goals


Creates a personalized career journey.


---

2️⃣ Aptitude & Interest Analysis

Evaluates:

Logical thinking

Technical inclination

Communication preference

Domain interest alignment


Generates intelligent recommendations.


---

3️⃣ Stage‑Aware Career Recommendations

Different suggestions based on user stage:

Class 12 Students

Recommended degree programs

Matching career streams

Required entrance exams


Bachelor's Students

Career role suggestions

Skill requirements

Higher study options


Master's Students

Specialized job roles

Industry‑focused preparation paths



---

4️⃣ Skill Gap Analyzer

Identifies:

Missing technical skills

Missing soft skills

Experience gaps


Provides improvement strategy.


---

5️⃣ Personalized Learning Roadmap

Creates structured plans including:

Skills to learn

Recommended timeline

Learning priorities

Progress tracking logic



---

6️⃣ Resume Analyzer

Analyzes resumes for:

ATS compatibility

Missing keywords

Structure issues

Improvement suggestions


Helps increase shortlist probability.


---

7️⃣ Mock Interview System

Simulates:

Technical interviews

HR interviews

Role‑specific questions


Improves confidence and readiness.


---

8️⃣ Career Readiness Score

Calculates readiness using:

Skills

Resume strength

Interview performance

Project experience


Outputs a measurable preparation score.
